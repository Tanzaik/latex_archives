\item The given differential equation is:\\
\begin{equation}
x''+6x'+25x=0 \qquad \qquad \qquad x(0)=2,\:x'(0)=-6
\end{equation}


\item The solution is:\\

\begin{equation}
x(t)=\sqrt{13}\:e^{-3t}\\
\left(\frac{2}{\sqrt{13}}\cos({4t})-\frac{3}{\sqrt{13}}\sin({4t})\right)\\
= \sqrt{13} e^{-3t}\cos({4t+0.9827})
\end{equation}
